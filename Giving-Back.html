Volunteer

Studies show that people who volunteer lead longer, healthier, and more fulfilling lives. Interested in volunteering, but don’t know where to start?

  • Do you enjoy reading to children?
  • Have you considered helping at a food shelf?
  • Do you knit, crochet or quilt and want to use your skill to benefit those in need?
  • Are you passionate about addressing the causes and conditions of poverty in your community?
  • Are you retired and want to put your life skills to work? Find out about volunteer opportunities at Brilliance-bridge by completing our quick one-page survey: Volunteer Interest Survey
